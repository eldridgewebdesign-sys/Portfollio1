[01] Our story

A workshop, a name, and one stubborn idea.

It began the way most good things do — small, and slightly against the advice of everyone sensible. A single bench, a borrowed lamp, and the conviction that things made carefully are worth waiting for. We have never seen a reason to change that.

For half a century the rule has stayed the same: take the time it takes. We have turned down work that would have made us bigger and worse, and kept the work that lets us put our name on the result without flinching.

"We would rather make fewer, and mean it."

The catalogue you are reading is short on purpose. Each entry is something we are glad to make again and again, refined quietly over the years rather than reinvented for the sake of a new season.

If a thing can be done by hand and done better, that is how we do it. The slow way is not nostalgia for its own sake — it is simply, still, the way that lasts.

— Set by hand · The Workshop · Established 1974
