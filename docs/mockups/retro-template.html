02The Story

We started in a back room with one good idea.

It began the way most useful things do — quietly, and a little stubbornly. A borrowed desk, a secondhand lamp, and a conviction that careful work still had a place. The first clients came by word of mouth, and they stayed because the work held up. That is the whole strategy, more or less.

Seasons passed. The desk got a little nicer; the principle did not change. We still answer our own messages, still sketch before we build, and still send the thing we are proud of rather than the thing that was due. If that sounds old-fashioned, we will take it as the compliment it is.

Today the practice is small on purpose. Small enough to know your name, large enough to do the work properly. We would rather be the studio you recommend twice than the one you forget once.

“Built to last. Priced to move.”
